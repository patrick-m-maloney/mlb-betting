POLYMARKET MLB MARKET RULES
============================

MARKETS WE WANT:
1. Game Moneylines
   Format: "{Away Team} vs. {Home Team}"
   Example: "Minnesota Twins vs. Boston Red Sox"
   
2. Season Win Totals
   Format: "Will the {Team} win more than {X} games in the 20XX..."
   Example: "Will the New York Yankees win more than 86.5 games in the 2026..."

MARKETS WE DO NOT WANT (ignore these):
- Spreads: any question starting with "Spread:"
- Over/Unders: any question containing "O/U"
- World Series futures: "Will the X win the 2026 World Series?"
- League/division champions: "win the 2026 AL/NL..."
- Division titles: "win the 2026 AL/NL East/West/Central title"
- Award markets: MVP, Cy Young, Hank Aaron Award
- WBC markets: "World Baseball Classic"
- Non-MLB sports: NHL, NBA, AHL etc.
- Single player props: "Will X hit the most..."

FIELDS NEEDED PER MARKET:
- question (title)
- condition_id
- yes_token_id, no_token_id (from clobTokenIds[0] and [1])
- yes_price, no_price (live from CLOB API)
- volume
- end_date
- market_type: 'game_winner' or 'win_total'
- home_team, away_team (parsed from question)
- win_total_threshold (for win total markets only, e.g. 86.5)
